How it works
This system helps you determinate the winner of euro 2016. OBS: The matches aren't ordered in Home/Away or dates.

The group are ordered in the following way:

  1. Higher number of points obtained in the matches played among the teams in question
  2. Superior goal difference resulting from the matches played among the teams in question
  3. higher number of goals scored in the matches played among the teams in question
  4. If, after having applied criteria 1) to 3), teams still have an equal ranking, criteria 1) to 3) are reapplied exclusively to the matches between the teams in question to determine their final rankings. If this procedure does not lead to a decision, criteria 5) to 8) apply
  5. Superior goal difference in all group matches
  6. Higher number of goals scored in all group matches;
  7. Fair play conduct in the final tournament
  8. Position in the UEFA national team coefficient rankings
